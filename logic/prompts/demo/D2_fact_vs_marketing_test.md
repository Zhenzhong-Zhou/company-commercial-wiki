# D2 — Fact vs marketing copy test

## Purpose

Check whether structured facts and marketing wording are separated cleanly.

## Prompt body

```text
For [PRODUCT], separate the vault content into:

A. structured product facts
B. marketing phrasing
C. high-risk customer-facing claims
D. unclear or unsupported claims

Use only existing wiki pages and source notes.
Show which page each item came from.
```
