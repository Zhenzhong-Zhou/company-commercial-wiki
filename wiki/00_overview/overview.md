# Company Commercial Knowledge Base Overview

## 中文标题
公司商业知识库总览

## Summary (EN)
This wiki is a structured commercial knowledge system for product information, material information, marketing messaging, translation and terminology, pricing, inquiry patterns, and customer-facing support content.

It is designed using a Raw / Wiki / Logic architecture:
- Raw = immutable source evidence
- Wiki = structured knowledge pages
- Logic = rules, agents, skills, templates, and lint checks

## 中文说明
本知识库用于管理公司商业相关知识，包括产品信息、物料信息、市场宣传、翻译术语、价格规则、问价模式与对外支持内容。

系统采用 Raw / Wiki / Logic 三层架构：
- Raw：原始资料层，不可随意改写
- Wiki：结构化知识页层
- Logic：运行逻辑层，包括 Agent、Rules、Skill、Template、Lint

## Core Domains
- Product Information
- Material Information
- Marketing Messaging
- Translation and Terminology
- Pricing and Quotation Rules
- Inquiry Patterns and Reply Templates

## Design Goals
- traceable
- bilingual-capable
- updateable
- archive-friendly
- English-first for system/schema/IT logic
- safer for customer-facing output

## Current Scope
v1 focuses on:
- company deck ingestion
- product marketing material ingestion
- bilingual terminology normalization
- source note creation
- first-generation product and pricing pages
