## Structured Facts
### Ingredients
- Vitamin D3 / 维生素 D3: 1000 IU per 2 tablets
- Vitamin K2 / 维生素 K2 (MK-7 microcapsule / MK-7 微囊): 60 mcg per 2 tablets
- Calcium bisglycinate / 双甘氨酸螯合钙: 790 mg per 2 tablets, providing elemental calcium 150 mg
- Magnesium bisglycinate / 双甘氨酸螯合镁: 384 mg per 2 tablets, providing elemental magnesium 50 mg
- Zinc bisglycinate / 双甘氨酸螯合锌: 46.66 mg per 2 tablets, providing elemental zinc 14 mg
