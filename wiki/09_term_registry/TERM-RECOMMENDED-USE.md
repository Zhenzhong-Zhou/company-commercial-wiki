# Recommended Use

## 中文名称
推荐用途

## Canonical English Term
Recommended Use

## Preferred Chinese Translation
推荐用途

## Aliases
- Use or purpose
- Recommended purpose
- 推荐用途 / 推荐目的

## Discouraged Variants
- Mixing recommended use with dosing instructions.
- Turning source-card marketing claims into regulator-approved recommended use.

## Domain
- product facts
- compliance
- translation
- customer-facing review

## Definition (EN)
Recommended Use means the intended purpose or benefit wording shown in source materials. It is separate from [[TERM-SUGGESTED-USE]], which describes how much and how often to take a product.

## 中文解释
推荐用途指资料中描述的用途或目的，不等同于食用方法。食用方法应记录在 [[TERM-SUGGESTED-USE]]，包括剂量、频次和年龄段。
