# Natural Product Number

## 中文名称
加拿大天然保健品编号

## Canonical English Term
Natural Product Number

## Preferred Chinese Translation
加拿大天然保健品编号

## Aliases
- NPN
- 加拿大 NPN
- NPN 编号

## Discouraged Variants
- "NPN production standard" when the meaning is a product license number.
- "NPN is stricter than FDA" as an unqualified customer-facing claim.
- "certification ceiling" or similar superiority language without approved substantiation.

## Domain
- compliance
- product facts
- marketing
- translation

## Definition (EN)
NPN is used in the source materials as a Canadian natural health product identifier. In this wiki it should be treated as a compliance-sensitive product reference, not as proof of every marketing claim unless the official record and permitted claims have been checked.

## 中文解释
NPN 在本资料库中作为加拿大天然保健品相关编号处理。它可以作为产品识别与合规核验线索，但不能自动证明所有营销功效或认证优越性表达。
