## Definition (EN)
Tablet is a dosage-form term used for compressed or tablet-form products. In this wiki, it should describe form only; stability or efficacy claims attached to tablet form require separate evidence.

## 中文解释
片剂用于描述压片或片状剂型。手卡中的干压片剂稳定性表达属于营销或材料主张，需要额外证据支持。
