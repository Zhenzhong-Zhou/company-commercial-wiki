# Suggested Use

## 中文名称
食用方法

## Canonical English Term
Suggested Use

## Preferred Chinese Translation
食用方法

## Aliases
- Directions
- Directions for use
- Suggested usage

## Discouraged Variants
- Recommended use, when the content is actually dose/frequency instructions.
- Medical dosing language unless supported by approved labeling.

## Domain
- product facts
- translation
- inquiry handling
- customer-facing review

## Definition (EN)
Suggested Use describes how a consumer is instructed to take the product, including age group, serving amount, frequency, timing, and relevant warnings. It is separate from [[TERM-RECOMMENDED-USE]].

## 中文解释
食用方法用于记录怎么吃、一次几粒、每天几次、适用年龄和注意事项。它不等同于推荐用途或功效描述。
